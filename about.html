About Gamrx



Hi, and welcome to the Gamrx about page!



gamrx.xyz was created on 8th of June 2024 and was made for only one purpose, to help the owner's Unity games be noticed by the community. This plan obviously took a long time to happen (mostly due to the fact that the owner's Unity games didn't exist yet) but that didn't stop me! I kept on trying and trying to get my games noticed by the community until today, when you are reading this (unless you know me personally) as a random person on the internet. Thank you for taking notice to gamrx.xyz and I hope you enjoy playing my games! Bye!
